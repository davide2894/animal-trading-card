Caretta Caretta

Loggerheads are members of the ancient family Cheloniidae, and appeared about 40 million years ago.

  • Alternate name: loggerhead sea turtle
  • Average Length: 90cm - 280cm
  • Average Lifespan: 47-67 years
  • Habitat: open ocean and shallow coastal waters

The loggerhead sea turtle is the world's largest hard-shelled turtle. Adults have an average weight range of 80 to 200 kg (180 to 440 lb) and a length range of 70 to 95 cm (28 to 37 in).[5] The maximum reported weight is 545 kg (1,202 lb) and the maximum carapace length is 213 cm (84 in).
